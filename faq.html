What services does Spark Electric currently offer?

Spark Electric currently focuses on smart home installation, low-voltage cabling, home networking, Wi-Fi, TV mounting, lighting, audio/video setup, troubleshooting, and residential technology installation.

Is Spark Electric insured?

Yes. Spark Electric is insured. Customers may request current documentation when needed for a project.

Is Spark Electric a licensed business?

Yes. Spark Electrical Services, LLC DBA Spark Electric is a licensed business.

What happens if a project requires a licensed electrical contractor or permit?

If a requested project appears to require a licensed electrical contractor, permit, or trade scope outside Spark Electric's current offering, that will be communicated before work is scheduled.

Which areas does Spark Electric serve?

Fairhope and Daphne are active focus areas. Baldwin County is the primary service area. Mobile, Spanish Fort, Foley, and Mobile County are planned or expanding markets unless confirmed for a specific request.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Call, text, email, or submit the contact form with your city, service type, photos, device models, and project details.

Can I provide my own devices?

Yes. Customer-provided devices are welcome when they are appropriate for the project. Sharing model numbers before the estimate helps confirm fit, compatibility, and installation needs.

Send your project details and Spark Electric will respond with the practical next step.
