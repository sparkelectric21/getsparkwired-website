What services does Spark Electric currently offer?

Spark Electric focuses on residential technology work: smart devices, lighting controls, networking, mounting, low-voltage cabling, troubleshooting, and setup.

Is Spark Electric insured?

Yes. Spark Electric is insured, and current documentation can be provided when a project requires it.

Is Spark Electric a licensed business?

Yes. Spark Electrical Services, LLC DBA Spark Electric is a licensed business.

What happens if a project requires a licensed electrical contractor or permit?

If a request appears to need a permit, licensed electrical contractor, or work outside current scope, Spark Electric will say so before scheduling.

Which areas does Spark Electric serve?

Baldwin County is the primary service area. Fairhope, Daphne, and Mobile County are active focus areas. Other nearby requests are reviewed by project and schedule.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Send your city, photos, device models, and a short description of what you want done.

Can I provide my own devices?

Yes, when the device fits the project. Model numbers help confirm compatibility before installation.
