Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

Spark Electric respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information submitted through our website and customer communications.

Information We Collect

When you request an estimate or contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, project details, and any other information you choose to provide.

How Information Is Used

We use submitted information to respond to estimate requests, answer questions, schedule service, provide customer support, and communicate about requested low-voltage, smart home, networking, lighting, or installation services.

Third-Party Form Processing

Quote requests are processed through Formsubmit. Information submitted through the form is sent to Spark Electric by email.

Email and Customer Communications

If you contact Spark Electric by form, phone, text, or email, we may use your contact information to respond and follow up about your project. We do not use customer contact information for unrelated email marketing.

Information Sharing

We do not sell customer information. We may share information only when needed to respond to your request, provide service, use necessary service providers, or comply with legal requirements.

Data Security

We use reasonable care to protect submitted information, but no website, email, or online transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Contact Information

For privacy questions, contact info@getsparkwired.com.
