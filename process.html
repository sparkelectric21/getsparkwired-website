How We Work

A clear path from project request through completion.

1. Tell us about your project.

Share what you want improved, your location, and any useful photos, model numbers, or room details.

2. We review the request.

We look at the project goal, location, equipment, existing conditions, and whether the work fits our current services and scope.

3. We clarify the work.

When more information is needed, we ask practical questions so the proposed work, important exclusions, and next decision are clear.

4. Approved work is prepared.

Before the project moves forward, the agreed scope, access needs, equipment, and customer preparation are confirmed as applicable.

5. The work is completed and reviewed.

We review what was completed, explain normal use when applicable, identify any open items, and give you an opportunity to ask questions.
